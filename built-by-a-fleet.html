Built by a Fleet

Reality2 is made the way Reality2 works — by a fleet of collaborating AI agents, guided by a human.

specialists collaborating · a human at the centre

A fleet of Claude AI agents builds this project — each a specialist in its own part of the system. They collaborate like a trust group: sharing context, checking one another's work, and converging on decisions.

A human stays at the centre as guardian — setting direction, reviewing the work, and holding the final say. No single agent owns the whole. Intelligence is distributed across many, trust is the connective tissue, and a person provides oversight and purpose.

Even this website was built this way.
